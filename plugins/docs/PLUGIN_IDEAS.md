## üéÆ Gamification & Engagement

### 1. **Message Streak Tracker**
- **Function**: Track daily message streaks (like Snapchat)
- **Features**: 
  - Visual streak counter in chat UI
  - Streak milestones and rewards
  - Integration with SoloLevelingStats (bonus XP for maintaining streaks)
- **Why**: Encourages daily engagement, pairs well with your Solo Leveling system

### 2. **Channel Activity Heatmap**
- **Function**: Visual heatmap showing your most active channels/times
- **Features**:
  - Calendar-style heatmap (like GitHub contributions)
  - Shows peak activity hours
  - Channel activity breakdown
- **Why**: Data visualization for your Discord habits

### 3. **Reaction Leaderboard**
- **Function**: Track who gives/receives most reactions
- **Features**:
  - Weekly/monthly leaderboards
  - Most reacted-to messages
  - Reaction statistics per user
- **Why**: Social engagement metrics

### 4. **Smart Message Templates**
- **Function**: Quick message templates with variables
- **Features**:
  - `/template greeting` ‚Üí expands to custom greeting
  - Variables: `{user}`, `{time}`, `{server}`
  - Context-aware templates (different per server/channel)
- **Why**: Saves time on repetitive messages

### 5. **Auto-Response Manager**
- **Function**: Smart auto-responses based on keywords/patterns
- **Features**:
  - Keyword triggers
  - Cooldown timers
  - Per-server/channel rules
  - Natural language matching
- **Why**: Handle common questions automatically

### 6. **Message Scheduler**
- **Function**: Schedule messages to send later
- **Features**:
  - Schedule for specific time/date
  - Recurring messages (daily reminders, etc.)
  - Queue management
- **Why**: Post at optimal times, never forget important messages

### 7. **Smart Channel Organizer**
- **Function**: Auto-organize channels based on activity
- **Features**:
  - Auto-mute inactive channels
  - Pin frequently used channels
  - Suggest channel cleanup
- **Why**: Keep server organized automatically
